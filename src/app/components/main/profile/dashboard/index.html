Tier levels

Tier levels description

Tier level 1 ✔

This is the entry-level tier, where users can enjoy an Instant Return On Investment (ROI) for the first year. This benefit is specifically tailored to cover the initial period, providing an immediate incentive for new users.

Tier level 2 ✔

Building upon the foundation of Tier 1, Tier 2 enhances the benefits package by not only offering an Instant ROI but also an Extra Interest of +2%. This tier is designed for users looking to increase their returns beyond the instant benefits, rewarding them for their continued engagement with the platform.

Tier level 3 ✔

The highest tier in the Credefi platform, Tier 3, includes all the benefits of the previous tiers—Instant ROI and an Extra Interest of +2%—and introduces Insurance Included. This additional feature offers a layer of protection for the users' investments, providing peace of mind and enhancing the overall value of being part of this tier.
